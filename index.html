Mortgage Calculator

This application creates an interface in which you are able to calculate your mortgage payments. You can enter the interest, loan, and duration of your loan. The calculator will display the monthly payments and the interest accumulated throughout the duration you have entered. It also calculates the remaining balance over the course of your loan.
